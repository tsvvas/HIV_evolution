Результаты:
1) Геном ВИЧ поделен на участки: более консервативные (БК) и менее консервативные.(As expected, some regions such as the variable loops in gp120 and nef evolve faster, while enzymes – protease (PR), reverse transcriptase (RT) – and the rev response element (RRE) evolve more slowly.)
	a)В БК участках наблюдается явное стремление к консенсусной последовательности (обратные мутации), что может свидетельствовать о том, что консенсус - sequence represents an ‘optimal’ HIV-1 sequence, which acts as an attractor for the evolutionary dynamics within hosts.
	b)В МК участках мутации менее зависимы от консенсуса
2) High recombination rate with average zone 100bp (Lack of long-range linkage due to frequent recombination)
3) Существует зависимость частот syn- и nonsyn-mutations от части генома (Indeed, we observe a strong anti-correlation between synonymous diversity and nonsynonymous divergence, which is further quantified in Figure 5C. This suggests that frequent non-synonymous substitutions limit synonymous diversity because they drive linked synonymous mutations to fixation or to extinction)
4) There should be some base-pattern for mutations (which can be modified for each patient)
5) There are selective sweeps in nonsynomous mutations (Do not fully understand this)
